Problem Title: Rectangle Area
Brute Force Method: Impossible to brute force via "counting pixels" for large coordinates. The mathematical approach is the default.
BF Complexity Visuals: Coordinate Geometry Plot.
How to Draw BF: Draw two overlapping rectangles on a Cartesian plane. Shade the union.
Optimised Pattern: Inclusion-Exclusion Principle (Area1 + Area2 - Overlap)
Opt Vis. Technique: 1D Projection Overlap
Dry Run (Opt): Project both rectangles onto the X-axis and Y-axis. The intersection area is (overlapX * overlapY).
How to Draw (Opt): Draw two horizontal lines (X-segments). Mark where they overlap. Do the same for vertical lines (Y-segments). The box formed by the overlaps is the intersection.
Opt Complexity Visuals: Constant Time Block: $O(1)$.
How to Draw Opt Complexity: Draw a single box. No loops, just arithmetic operations. T = constant.
Mem Vis (BF): 8 Variable Boxes for coordinates.
Mem Vis (Opt): Fixed Variables: Draw 4 "max/min" boxes to calculate the overlap boundaries.
