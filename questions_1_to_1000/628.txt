Problem Title: Maximum Product of Three Numbers
Brute Force Method: Three nested loops to find the product of every possible triplet in the array.
BF Complexity Visuals: 3D Volume Grid (N x N x N).
How to Draw BF: Draw an array. Point to 3 different numbers. Show how many combinations exist by drawing a dense cube of dots. $O(N^3)$.
Optimised Pattern: Sort OR Linear Scan for Top 3 and Bottom 2.
Opt Vis. Technique: Extreme Bound Tracking.
Dry Run (Opt): Find 3 largest numbers and 2 smallest (most negative). Max product is either (Max1*Max2*Max3) or (Max1*Min1*Min2).
How to Draw (Opt): Draw a horizontal line. Mark 3 dots at the far right and 2 dots at the far left. Draw two "product" bubbles comparing the results.
Opt Complexity Visuals: $O(N \log N)$ for sorting or $O(N)$ for single-pass scan.
How to Draw Opt Complexity: Draw a scanner moving across the array once. Write "Min1, Min2, Max1, Max2, Max3" above it. $O(N)$.
Mem Vis (BF): Draw a massive list of all calculated products.
Mem Vis (Opt): Draw exactly five integer variables. $O(1)$ space.
