ID: 649
Problem Title: Dota2 Senate
Brute Force Method: Repeatedly scan the string, marking "banned" senators, until only one party remains. Use a boolean array for "active" status.
BF Complexity Visuals: Circular multi-pass scan.
How to Draw BF: Draw a circle of 'R' and 'D'. Cross one out, move forward, repeat until only one color is left. Write $O(N^2)$ for many passes.
Optimised Pattern: Two Queues (R_index, D_index).
Opt Vis. Technique: Queue-based Race / Index Bumping.
Dry Run (Opt): Put indices of 'R' in `queueR` and 'D' in `queueD`. Compare front of both. Smaller index "wins" and is re-added to its queue with index + N. The loser is discarded.
How to Draw (Opt): Draw two separate lines (Radiant and Dire). The person at the front of line R battles person at front of line D. The winner goes to the back of their line.
Opt Complexity Visuals: Single pass through elements, re-queuing each at most once.
How to Draw Opt Complexity: Draw two parallel lines merging/looping. Write $O(N)$.
Mem Vis (BF): Draw multiple copies of the string for state tracking.
Mem Vis (Opt): Draw two Queues storing integers (indices). $O(N)$ space.
