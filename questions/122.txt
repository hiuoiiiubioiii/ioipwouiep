Problem Title: Best Time to Buy and Sell Stock II
Brute Force Method: Recursion to explore every possible combination of buying, selling, or holding on every single day.
BF Complexity Visuals: A massive branching decision tree with $O(2^N)$ states.
How to Draw BF: Draw a decision tree: "Day 1: Buy or Skip?", "Day 2: Sell, Hold, or Skip?". Show it exploding exponentially.
Optimised Pattern: Greedy Algorithm (Summing Positive Slopes).
Opt Vis. Technique: A mountain climber collecting only the upward inclines. If tomorrow is higher than today, take the profit immediately.
Dry Run (Opt): Traverse prices from index 1. If prices[i] &gt; prices[i-1], add the difference to total profit.
How to Draw (Opt): Draw a price graph. Circle every upward-sloping line segment. Draw "+" signs over them and a total sum box at the end.
Opt Complexity Visuals: Linear scan with no nested dependencies.
How to Draw Opt Complexity: Draw the array. Draw a single pass. Highlight only the adjacent pairs where the second element is larger. $O(N)$ time.
Mem Vis (BF): $O(2^N)$ recursive call stack frames leading to total system exhaustion.
Mem Vis (Opt): A single $O(1)$ accumulator variable updating as you step through the list.
