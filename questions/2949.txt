How to Draw (Opt): Draw a state-transition diagram where each index updates its (diff, mod) pair.
Opt Complexity Visuals: Linear Pass with Math Precomputation
How to Draw Opt Complexity: Draw a line of length N. T = $O(N + √k)$.
