Problem Title: Minimum Number of Operations to Make Arrays Similar
Brute Force Method: Recursively try +/- 2 on every element to match the target array. $O(\text{Exponential})$.
BF Complexity Visuals: Exploding +/- 2 Tree
How to Draw BF: Draw a number. Branch it up by 2 and down by 2 infinitely until it hits a target.
Optimised Pattern: Sorting + Parity Segregation
Opt Vis. Technique: The Odd/Even Parallel Tracks
Dry Run (Opt): Since you can only add/sub 2, evens stay even, odds stay odd. Split both arrays into Odd and Even lists. Sort all 4 lists. Compare them index by index, summing positive differences and dividing by 2.
How to Draw (Opt): Draw two funnels: "Evens" and "Odds". Pour nums inside. Sort them. Draw straight horizontal lines connecting `sorted_nums[i]` to `sorted_target[i]`.
Opt Complexity Visuals: Sorting Bottleneck $O(N \log N)$
How to Draw Opt Complexity: Draw a merge-sort tree, followed by two parallel linear scans.
Mem Vis (BF): Deep recursive stack.
Mem Vis (Opt): Two separate arrays for Odds and Evens. $O(N)$ space.
