ID: 3199
Problem Title: Count Triplets with Even XOR Sum
Brute Force Method: Triple nested loops to iterate through all combinations of three arrays and calculate XOR. $O(N^3)$.
BF Complexity Visuals: 3D Voxel Grid of Comparisons
How to Draw BF: Draw 3 arrays. Draw lines connecting every i, j, k. Tally if val1 oplus val2 oplus val3 is even.
Optimised Pattern: Bitwise Parity Counting
Opt Vis. Technique: Even/Odd Probability Buckets
Dry Run (Opt): Count even and odd numbers in each array. Triplets with even XOR sum must have 0 or 2 odd numbers. Calculate using combinations of these counts.
How to Draw (Opt): Draw three boxes (A, B, C). In each, write the count of `even` and `odd`. Multiply the combinations that yield an even sum.
Opt Complexity Visuals: Discrete Scalar Math
How to Draw Opt Complexity: Draw a small formula box. Label it $O(N)$.
Mem Vis (BF): Massive list of all triplet XOR results.
Mem Vis (Opt): Six integer variables (2 per array). $O(1)$.
