Problem Title: Maximum Absolute Sum of Any Subarray
Brute Force Method: Generate all subarrays, calculate their sums, take absolute values, and find the maximum.
BF Complexity Visuals: Nested Subarray Matrix.
How to Draw BF: Draw array. Draw brackets for every possible start and end. Calculate sums. $O(N^2)$.
Optimised Pattern: Prefix Sum Range (Max - Min).
Opt Vis. Technique: Vertical Oscillation (Altitude Swing).
Dry Run (Opt): The max absolute subarray sum is simply the difference between the maximum prefix sum and the minimum prefix sum (including 0).
How to Draw (Opt): Draw a line graph of prefix sums. Find the highest peak and the lowest valley. The vertical distance between them is your answer.
Opt Complexity Visuals: Single Pass Min-Max Tracking.
How to Draw Opt Complexity: Draw one arrow sweeping the array, updating `max_pre` and `min_pre`. Label $O(N)$.
Mem Vis (BF): None, just sum variables.
Mem Vis (Opt): Two integer variables: `min_sum` and `max_sum`. $O(1)$ space.
