ID: 2958
Problem Title: Length of Longest Subarray With at Most K Frequency
Brute Force Method: Generate all $O(N^2)$ subarrays and check if every element's frequency <= k.
BF Complexity Visuals: Quadratic Matrix Area
How to Draw BF: Draw a triangle representing N^2/2 operations. Label "TLE" for N=10^5.
Optimised Pattern: Sliding Window (Two Pointers)
Opt Vis. Technique: Expanding/Contracting Accordion
Dry Run (Opt): Move `right` pointer to include elements. If frequency of `nums[right] &gt; k`, move `left` pointer forward until the frequency is restored to k.
How to Draw (Opt): Draw an array. Draw a bracket `[ ]`. Show the bracket expanding right, then "shrinking" from the left when a frequency limit is hit.
Opt Complexity Visuals: Linear Sweep (2N total moves)
How to Draw Opt Complexity: Draw a line of length N. Both `left` and `right` move only forward. T = $O(N)$.
Mem Vis (BF): Storing N^2 frequency maps.
Mem Vis (Opt): A Hash Map tracking frequencies of elements in the current window. Space $O(N)$.
