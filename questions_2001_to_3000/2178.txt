Problem Title: Maximum Split of Positive Even Integers
Brute Force Method: Recursively try every combination of even integers to see which set sums to N.
BF Complexity Visuals: A subset-sum style decision tree.
How to Draw BF: Draw N. Branch into 2, 4, 6, 8... For each, branch again. $O(2^N)$.
Optimised Pattern: Greedy (Smallest Even Strategy).
Opt Vis. Technique: Filling a container with the smallest possible even blocks first.
Dry Run (Opt): If N is odd, return empty. Otherwise, take 2, 4, 6... until the remaining sum is less than the next even number. Add that remainder to the last number in the list.
How to Draw (Opt): Draw a bar of length N. Cut a piece of length 2. Cut a piece of length 4. Keep cutting until you can't cut a piece larger than the previous. Take the "scrap" and weld it onto the last piece.
Opt Complexity Visuals: $O(\text{sqrt}(N)$) because the sum of 2+4+...+2k grows quadratically.
How to Draw Opt Complexity: A staircase that grows until its height matches the target.
Mem Vis (BF): Massive recursion stack for backtracking.
Mem Vis (Opt): $O(\text{sqrt}(N)$) to store the resulting list.
