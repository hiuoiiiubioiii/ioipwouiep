ID: 2335
Problem Title: Minimum Amount of Time to Fill Cups
Brute Force Method: Recursively try every combination of filling two cups or one cup at a time, branching out to find the path with the minimum depth to reach `[0,0,0]`.
BF Complexity Visuals: Exponential Pouring Tree (O(3^Sum))
How to Draw BF: Draw an array of 3 numbers. Draw branches for decrementing (0,1), (1,2), (0,2), or just one alone. Repeat until all reach zero.
Optimised Pattern: Greedy Math / Max Priority Queue
Opt Vis. Technique: Leveling the Two Tallest Towers
Dry Run (Opt): Sort the 3 numbers. Always decrement the two largest numbers. If the sum of the two smaller ones is less than the largest, the answer is just the largest. Otherwise, math trick: `ceil((a+b+c)/2)`.
How to Draw (Opt): Draw 3 stacks of coins. Take one coin from the tallest stack and one from the second tallest. Repeat. If one stack is empty, just take from the tallest remaining.
Opt Complexity Visuals: $O(1)$ Math Block (or $O(\text{Sum})$ PQ loop)
How to Draw Opt Complexity: Draw 3 numbers instantly transforming into the final answer via one math equation.
Mem Vis (BF): Draw a deep Call Stack holding arrays of size 3 in various stages of depletion.
Mem Vis (Opt): Draw a Priority Queue (size 3) or literally just zero extra memory using the $O(1)$ math formula.
