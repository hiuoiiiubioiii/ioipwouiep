Problem Title: Minimum Cost to Make Array Equalindromic
Brute Force Method: Check every possible palindrome number from 1 to max(nums) and calculate the cost for each.
BF Complexity Visuals: Infinite Linear Search
How to Draw BF: Draw a number line. At every integer, calculate sum |nums[i] - p|. $O(\text{Range} \cdot N)$.
Optimised Pattern: Median + Palindrome Generation
Opt Vis. Technique: Mountain Peak Targeting
Dry Run (Opt): The optimal target must be near the median. Find the median M. Generate the closest palindromes above and below M. Calculate costs for both and take min.
How to Draw (Opt): Draw a bell curve of costs. Mark the median M at the bottom. Draw two nearby points (palindromes) and compare their "heights" (costs).
Opt Complexity Visuals: Sorting + Constant Search
How to Draw Opt Complexity: Draw a line of length N. T = $O(N \log N)$ to find the median. Palindrome generation is $O(\text{digits})$.
Mem Vis (BF): No extra memory.
Mem Vis (Opt): A few long long variables for median and palindromes. Space $O(1)$.
