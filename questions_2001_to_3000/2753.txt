Problem Title: Count Houses in a Circular Street II
Brute Force Method: Randomly walk around the circle and count houses until you think you've seen them all. No guarantee of correctness.
BF Complexity Visuals: Infinite Random Walk
How to Draw BF: Draw a circle. Draw a person wandering aimlessly. The path line never closes or terminates predictably.
Optimised Pattern: Unbounded Step Counter.
Opt Vis. Technique: Marker Placement &amp; Seek (O(k) where k is max possible)
Dry Run (Opt): Flag and Lap Sweep
How to Draw (Opt): Close the door at the start. Move k houses (max limit), closing all doors as you go. Then open the door at the start. Walk until you hit that open door. The number of steps is the count.
Opt Complexity Visuals: Draw a circle of houses. Draw a hand closing every door in one lap. Open one door. Walk until you find that "Flag" door again.
How to Draw Opt Complexity: Linear $O(k)$
Mem Vis (BF): Draw two circles representing the two laps. Label it $O(k)$.
Mem Vis (Opt): $O(1)$ State Variables (Draw one integer steps).
