Problem Title: Count Palindromic Subsequences
Brute Force Method: Generate all 5-length subsequences and check if they are palindromes. $O(N^5)$.
BF Complexity Visuals: Quintic Expansion Grid
How to Draw BF: Draw 5 nested loops represented by 5 overlapping brackets. Practically unrunnable.
Optimised Pattern: Prefix/Suffix DP (Length 5)
Opt Vis. Technique: The Pivot Expansion
Dry Run (Opt): A 5-palindrome looks like `xy_yx`. Iterate through every index as the center `_`. Count how many times the prefix has the sequence `xy` and the suffix has the sequence `yx`. Multiply them.
How to Draw (Opt): Draw the string. Pick a middle letter. Look Left: Count occurrences of "12", "13", etc. Look Right: Count occurrences of "21", "31". Multiply the matches.
Opt Complexity Visuals: Linear Scan with 100-State Check $O(N \cdot 100)$
How to Draw Opt Complexity: Draw a timeline. At each step, update a fixed 10x10 grid of 2-digit combinations.
Mem Vis (BF): Full subset generation arrays. $O(N^5)$.
Mem Vis (Opt): Prefix and Suffix 3D arrays tracking 2-digit counts. $O(N \cdot 100)$ -&gt; $O(N)$ space.
